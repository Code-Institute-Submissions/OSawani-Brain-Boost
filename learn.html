The Lifeline We Didn't Know We Needed

Remember the last time you learned something new and felt that rush? That's your brain, forming new connections, growing stronger, resisting the digital drain. It's telling you, "More, please!" So, where do we go from here?

Growth mindset & neural connections
• Evidence: Embracing a growth mindset can lead to enhanced motivation and academic performance. A paper in "Psychological Science" delves into how a growth mindset shapes the anatomy of the brain's connectivity. Psychological Science Study on Growth Mindset

Learning new skills like a sport or playing an instrument:
• Evidence: Acquiring new motor skills improves connectivity between brain regions. A study in "JNeurosci" explained the enhancement of white matter structure in the brain following motor skill learning. JNeurosci Study on Motor Skill Learning

Blueberries aka Brain-Berries
• Evidence: Challenging the brain with memory tasks can bolster neural plasticity and cognitive resilience. A study from "Radiology" showcased how memory training interventions modulate brain function and structure. Radiology Study on Memory Training
