Are You Ready to Elevate Your Brain with Movement?

Every step you take, every breath you make can amplify your cognitive prowess. Dive into proven practices: restorative sleep, nature walks, and controlled breathing. Tired? Refresh with a stroll in green spaces or meditative moments. From the "Journal of Neuroscience" to "PLOS ONE", harness the best strategies for a sharper you.

Sleeping & regulating biological hours
• Evidence: Sleep plays a pivotal role in memory consolidation and brain detoxification. A study from the "Journal of Neuroscience" elucidated how sleep deprivation impairs hippocampal function and memory. Journal of Neuroscience Study on Sleep

Walking outside & exercise
• Evidence: Walking, especially in green spaces, has been shown to reduce mental fatigue and increase creativity. A study in "PLOS ONE" highlighted how nature experience reduces rumination and subgenual prefrontal cortex activation. PLOS ONE Study on Nature Walks

Regulating your breathing while exercising
• Evidence: Controlled breathing, especially during yoga and meditation, has been shown to reduce stress, enhance cortical thickness, and improve attention. A study in "Frontiers in Human Neuroscience" details the impact of yogic breathing on brain structure. Frontiers in Human Neuroscience Study on Yogic Breathing
